A photo shoot is a series of images that are taken, with the goal of obtaining images that can then be placed into post-production, or editing. These images are then used for print/digital advertising, business collateral, or just for personal use.

Process taken by creatives and models that results in a visual objective being obtained. An example is a model posing for a photographer at a studio or an outdoor location.

PHOTO PROCESS

1. Acquaintance

In order for our shooting to be easy and fun, we need to get to know each other better.

2. Concept

We discuss all the details of the shooting. We choose a location, theme, and image, select references.

3. Photography

During the shooting, I will advise you on all issues. If necessary, there will be a change of location and image.

4. Ready photos

After the agreed time, you will receive finished photos processed, taking into account your wishes.
